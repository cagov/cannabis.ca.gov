New Reference Guides for Cannabis Cultivation License Applicants

Hello CalCannabis Cultivation Licensing Stakeholders:
To assist those of you who are applying for an annual commercial cannabis cultivation license, we have created a couple of new reference guides to help explain what’s required for the cultivation plan and some of the other required application attachments.

May 2, 2018
Applications for Shared-Use Cannabis Manufacturing Facilities Are Now Available

Emergency regulations for shared-use cannabis manufacturing facilities, released by the California Department of Public Health, have been approved and are now in effect.

April 18, 2018
Notice Regarding Temporary Cannabis Events

Please be aware that lawful participation by bureau licensees in any temporary cannabis event that allows sales and/or consumption is dependent upon issuance of the appropriate licenses from the bureau.

April 17, 2018
Bureau of Cannabis Control – Commercial Cannabis Licensee Bond Form

One of the requirements for the Bureau of Cannabis Control’s annual license application is to provide proof of having obtained a surety bond of at least $5,000, payable to the State of California.

April 16, 2018
Cannabis Cultivation Licensees: Compliance & Enforcement Inspections Are In Progress

We would like to remind all state-licensed cannabis cultivators that the Compliance and Enforcement branch of CalCannabis Cultivation Licensing, a division of the California Department of Food and Agriculture (CDFA), is now conducting inspections across the state.
