California Cannabis Advisory Committee Publishes 2018 Annual Report

California’s Cannabis Advisory Committee (CAC) recently published their full 2018 Annual Report to the Bureau of Cannabis Control’s website.

Notice Regarding Temporary Cannabis Cultivation License Applications

Attention CalCannabis Cultivation Licensing Stakeholders: On December 31, 2018, the authority for the California Department of Food and Agriculture (CDFA) to issue temporary cannabis cultivation licenses expired.

Results of Cannabis Banking Feasibility Study to be Released at Public Hearing

California’s 18-member Cannabis Banking Working Group (CBWG), chaired by State Treasurer John Chiang, will hold a public hearing Thursday, December 27, where they will be presented with the results of the cannabis banking feasibility study prepared by Level 4 Venture.

California Department of Tax and Fee Administration – Adoption of Proposed Emergency Cannabis Tax Regulation 3702

The California Department of Tax and Fee Administration (CDTFA) has adopted proposed emergency Cannabis Tax Regulation 3702, California Cannabis Track-and-Trace, to require the wholesale cost and retail selling price of cannabis and cannabis products to be recorded in the California Cannabis Track-and-Trace (CCTT) system.

Bureau of Cannabis Control – Testing Requirements for Cannabis Goods Reminder

The Bureau has been receiving questions about which cannabis goods are subject to the phase three testing requirements contained in section 5715 of the Bureau’s regulations.
