Bureau of Cannabis Control Announces Enforcement Action on Unlicensed Delivery Service in Sacramento

The Bureau of Cannabis Control and the Department of Consumer Affairs’ Division of Investigation-Cannabis Enforcement Unit today announced enforcement action on an unlicensed cannabis business operating in Sacramento.

Bureau of Cannabis Control Announces Enforcement Action on Unlicensed Cannabis Operator

The Bureau of Cannabis Control and the Department of Consumer Affairs’ Division of Investigation-Cannabis Enforcement Unit, in coordination with the Costa Mesa Police Department and local agencies, today announced enforcement action on an unlicensed cannabis business operating in Costa Mesa.

Cannabis Control Appeals Panel Website

The Cannabis Control Appeals Panel is responsible for appeals of any decision by state cannabis licensing authorities relating to the order of any penalty assessment, issuing, denying, transferring, conditioning, suspending or revoking any license provided under MAUCRSA.

Bureau of Cannabis Control to Hold Public Hearing on Proposed Cannabis Regulations in Sacramento

SACRAMENTO – The Bureau of Cannabis Control (Bureau) announced today that the final hearing of the 45-day public comment period for our proposed non-emergency cannabis regulations will be held on Monday, August 27 in Sacramento.
