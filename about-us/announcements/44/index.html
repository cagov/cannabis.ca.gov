Notice Regarding the Repeal of Health and Safety Code Section 11362.775 Medical Marijuana Program – Collectives or Cooperatives

Health and Safety Code section 11362.775, provides that it shall remain in effect only until one year after the Bureau of Cannabis Control posts a notice on its Internet Web site that the state licensing authorities have commenced issuing licenses pursuant to the Medicinal and Adult-Use Cannabis Regulation and Safety Act.
