SACRAMENTO – The Bureau of Cannabis Control (Bureau) today announced it has approved the issuance of annual licenses for 12 commercial cannabis businesses in California. The businesses must first pay their licensing fee before their annual license will be issued and active. Annual licenses issued by the Bureau are effective for 12 months.

To date, nearly 14,000 users have registered with the Bureau’s online system, and almost 4,000 applications have been submitted. As of November 2, the Bureau has issued more than 1,200 active temporary licenses. To search for cannabis businesses that have been issued temporary or annual licenses by location and license type, please visit our website’s ‘Check a License’ page: http://capotcheck.com/

Those interested in applying for a commercial cannabis license can access the Bureau’s online licensing system at http://online.bcc.ca.gov/.
