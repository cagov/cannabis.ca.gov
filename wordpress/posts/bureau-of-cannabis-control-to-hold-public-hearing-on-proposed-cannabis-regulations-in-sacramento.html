SACRAMENTO – The Bureau of Cannabis Control (Bureau) announced today that the final hearing of the 45-day public comment period for our proposed non-emergency cannabis regulations will be held on Monday, August 27 in Sacramento. The hearing is intended to provide the public with an opportunity to voice their opinions on the rulemaking process.

During the hearing, the public is welcome to present oral comments or submit written comments in person as it pertains to the Bureau’s proposed regulations.

BUREAU OF CANNABIS CONTROL – PUBLIC HEARING IN SACRAMENTO:

WHERE: Tsakopoulos Library Galleria, 828 I Street, Sacramento, CA 95814

WHEN: Monday, August 27, 2018, 10:00 a.m. to Noon

Oral comments given at the hearing will be recorded by the Bureau. To ensure that all hearing attendees have an opportunity to provide oral comments, each speaker may be limited to a specific amount of time to comment. Those who wish to provide an oral comment may also wish to bring a written comment to submit at the hearing.

LIVE WEBCAST: The Bureau also plans to webcast this public hearing. To view the webcast, please visit the following link on August 27 at the hearing’s scheduled start time: https://thedcapage.blog/webcasts/.

SEATING LIMITED: Meeting attendees are encouraged to arrive early, as the Bureau cannot guarantee seating in the event the room reaches capacity.
