SACRAMENTO – The Bureau of Cannabis Control (Bureau) today announced that the Cannabis Advisory Committee (CAC) will hold its first meeting of the year on Wednesday, March 27 in Sacramento. The Meeting Notice and Agenda has been posted to the Bureau’s website and can be found here – http://bcc.ca.gov/about_us/committee_meetings.html.

CANNABIS ADVISORY COMMITTEE MEETING:

WHERE: Hilton Sacramento Arden West – Grand Ballroom, 2200 Harvard Street, Sacramento, CA 95815

WHEN: Wednesday, March 27, 2019 at 10 a.m.

LIVE WEBCAST: The Bureau also plans to webcast this meeting live. To view the CAC meeting webcast, please visit the following link on March 27 at the meeting’s scheduled start time – https://thedcapage.blog/webcasts/.

SEATING LIMITED: Meeting attendees are encouraged to arrive early Bureau cannot guarantee seating in the event the room reaches capacity.

The CAC advises the Bureau and the other licensing authorities – the California Department of Food and Agriculture and the California Department of Public Health – on the development of regulations that help protect public health and safety and reduce the illegal market for cannabis.

For additional information about the Cannabis Advisory Committee, or to subscribe to email alerts to hear about updates as they become available, please visit the Bureau’s website at http://www.bcc.ca.gov/. For information on all three state licensing authorities, please visit the state’s California Cannabis Portal at https://cannabis.ca.gov/. Follow the Bureau on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram for daily news and updates.
