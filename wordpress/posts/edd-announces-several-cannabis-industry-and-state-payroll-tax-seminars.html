The Employment Development Department (EDD) offers State payroll tax seminars to help employers comply with California payroll tax laws. During their upcoming Cannabis Industry and State Payroll Tax Seminars, interested parties will learn how and when to report State payroll taxes, how to distinguish between an employee and an independent contractor, independent contractor reporting requirements, and the various ways to get assistance from EDD.

Please note, registration is required to attend these seminars. For a full list of dates and locations for all 26 seminars through the end of the year, visit EDD’s website. See below for a shortened list of seminars available through the month of August.
