This is California Cannabis (2020 to present)

This is California Cannabis focuses on cultivation and cannabis farmers. The campaign:

  • Promotes the legal cannabis cultivation market
  • Highlights hardworking cannabis farmers who drive the industry forward
  • Promotes the benefits of a strong and safe legal market

The campaign includes:

  • Billboards
  • Online advertising
  • Social media posts
  • Email newsletters
  • Videos
Learn more at This is California Cannabis

Get #weedwise (2019 to present)

Get #weedwise educates consumers about:

  • The risks associated with illicit products
  • How to identify a licensed retailer

It also warns businesses of the consequences of operating without a license.

The campaign includes:

  • Billboards
  • Online advertising
  • Social media posts
  • Presentations
Learn more at Get #weedwise
