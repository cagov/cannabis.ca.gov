SACRAMENTO – The Bureau of Cannabis Control (Bureau) announced today that its North Coast Regional Office will open in its new location on Tuesday, April 16, 2019. The new office is located at 930 Sixth Street in Eureka, CA and will be open Monday through Friday from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. 

“The opening of this larger office allows us to provide greater service to our applicants and licensees in the North Coast Region,” Bureau Chief Lori Ajax said. “One of the important benefits of this office is that the public can come to one location and have access to staff from four state agencies involved in the commercial cannabis licensing process.”

The office will be shared by the Bureau, the California Department of Food and Agriculture, the California Department of Public Health, and the California Water Resources Control Board. Members of the public may pay their fees, submit license applications, and have questions answered in person at this location.
