The “This is California Cannabis” campaign features vibrant and compelling videos, photographs, and other material that helps tell the stories of our community of licensees and the benefits of the legal cannabis industry. These creative assets are targeted to reach licensees and other stakeholders statewide through billboards, online digital ads, social media, and radio.

If you are interested in accessing our digital ads, 30-second public-service radio announcements, billboard art, social-media content, or videos to use in your own communications and media campaigns, please see the shareable content below or contact us by sending an email to outreach@cannabis.ca.gov.

Campaign toolkits

We need your help. Join us in celebrating the dedicated cannabis business owners and our committed partners who together are moving the state’s legal cannabis industry forward. Each of the toolkits listed below — one geared toward cannabis licensees and one for other stakeholders — offers in-depth information about the campaign, shareable content and assets, and useful resources to help you take part in “This is California Cannabis.”

Licensee profiles

At the core of our “This is California Cannabis” campaign is the community of dedicated, innovative licensees who are paving the way for generations to come. Do you know outstanding licensed cannabis operators who deserve to be recognized? We’d like to feature these exceptional stories in our newsletter, social media posts, videos, and ad campaigns. Please send an email to outreach@cannabis.ca.gov with details about the license holder and a DCC communications team member will respond.
