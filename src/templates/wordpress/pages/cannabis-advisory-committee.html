The Cannabis Advisory Committee (CAC) are subject-matter experts that advise the Department of Cannabis Control (DCC) on regulations that:

  • Protect public health and safety
  • Reduce the illegal cannabis market

Committee members

DCC is recruiting for advisory committee members. Applications were collected from December 2021 until January 14, 2022. The application window is now closed.

Annual reports

Each year the committee issues a report that describes:

  • What they did during the previous year
  • Recommendations made
  • Whether recommendations were implemented
